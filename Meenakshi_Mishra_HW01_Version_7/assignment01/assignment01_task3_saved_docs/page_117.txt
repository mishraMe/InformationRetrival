<p><b>Gonghe County</b> (in Tibetan, <b>Kungho</b>) is a county of <a href="/wiki/Qinghai" title="Qinghai">Qinghai Province</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a> under the administration of <a href="/wiki/Hainan_Tibetan_Autonomous_Prefecture" title="Hainan Tibetan Autonomous Prefecture">Hainan Prefecture</a>. The seat of Gonghe County is in the town of <a href="/wiki/Qabqa" title="Qabqa">Qabqa</a> (<span lang="zh" xml:lang="zh">恰卜恰</span> <i>Qiàbǔqià</i>). Many people in this and surrounding Tibetan Autonomous Prefectures refer to the place by its Tibetan name, Chabcha (ཆབ་ཆ།).</p>
